About the Hackathon
We are proud to keep this tradition going by hosting the 3rd Hackathon. The CSX Hackathon 2019 will take place at CSX Technology 550 building on May 17th & May 18th. In order to attend we are asking all the participants to register. The teams can't be bigger than 5 members. If you don't have a team just type in the registration form “N/A” as your team.
